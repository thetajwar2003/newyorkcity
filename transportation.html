Transportation

There are many ways to get around New York City. One way is the subway station. New York City has tons of different subways that connect each borough. Just take a look at the map below. The subway uses MetroCards. You must use these cards to swipe through readers in order to enter the platform. You can buy these MetroCards from a vending machine. The cost of a MetroCard is from $2.50-$2.75 for a single ride; the cost of a seven-day unlimited MetroCard from $30 to $31; and the cost of a monthly unlimited MetroCard from $112 to $116.50

Another way to get around NYC is to use the bus. Take a look at the picture below. The public bus also uses the MetroCard. If you don't want to pay $1.00 for every new MetroCard, don't lose the card. And be sure to refill the card whenever you have a low balance.
