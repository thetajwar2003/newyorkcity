The Hudson Hotel

It's walking distance from Central Park, the Time Warner Center, Lincoln Center and Times Square, but sits on unassuming 58th and 8th, out of the lion’s den. It is located in 358 West 58th Street, Hell's Kitchen, New York City, NY 10019, United States of America

You can order a burger and truffle fries at the window, then grab a seat on a comfy leather chair or at a long wood communal table; and the Library Bar is perfect for reading by yourself or playing a game of pool. The Hudson also hosts periodic events and popups popular with locals and tourists alike, like a minigolf course on the patio and Gatsby-themed New Year’s Eve party. For more information, click here
